How to Activate Your Lowe’s Credit Card at lowes.syf.com/activate?

Follow these simple steps to activate your Lowe’s Synchrony card online:

  1. Go to lowes.syf.com/activate.
  2. Enter your credit card number and other required personal details.
  3. Click “Activate”.
  4. Once verified, your Lowe’s credit card will be ready to use.

My Lowe’s Credit Card Account Sign In

After activation, you can access your Lowe’s credit card account using the login portal:

  • Visit: lowes.syf.com
  • Click on “Sign In”.
  • Enter your User ID and Password.

This portal is also known as the Lowes Synchrony Card Log In page. Here, you can:

  • View transactions
  • Check available balance
  • Track rewards
  • Make credit card payments

Lowes Credit Card Payment – Easy & Secure Methods

Online:

  • Sign in to your account at lowes.syf.com.
  • Navigate to the payment section.
  • Set up one-time or automatic payments.

Other Payment Methods:

  • Phone payment (automated service)
  • Mail-in payment
  • In-store payment at any Lowe’s location

Why Create a Lowe’s Credit Login Account?

Your Lowe’s credit login gives you full control over your account. With online access, you can:

  • Manage multiple Lowe’s credit cards
  • Update personal and billing information
  • Set up alerts and autopay
  • Redeem offers and discounts

Need to Reset Lowe’s Credit Card Login Password?

  1. Go to the lowes.syf.com login page.
  2. Click “Forgot User ID or Password?”
  3. Enter your card number and billing ZIP code.
  4. Follow prompts to reset your login.
